Sound Familiar?

You run a great giving day. But what about the other 364?

Your giving day is a highlight of the year. But if you're like most community foundations, the platform you use was built for that one event — and the rest of the year, you're on your own.

"We only use our platform one day a year"

You're paying for a giving day tool that sits idle for months. Meanwhile, your nonprofits need year-round fundraising support and your donors want to give when they're inspired — not just on one designated day.

"Our smaller nonprofits struggle to participate"

A handful of well-resourced organizations raise most of the money. Smaller nonprofits — the ones who need your giving day most — don't have the capacity to build campaigns, tell their story, or follow up with donors.

"We're paying too much for too little"

Between platform license fees and processing costs, you're spending 5-7% of every dollar raised. That's money your nonprofits and donors expect to go to the causes they care about.
